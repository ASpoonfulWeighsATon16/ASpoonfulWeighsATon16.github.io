Proprietor Nation: The Rise of the Gig Economy

Since 1970, the nation's number of proprietors, or self-employed individuals, has increased by 329 percent. In comparison, the number of people working for wages increased by just 112 percent. However, the average income earned by proprietors is often erratic from year-to-year. Explore these changes to our nation of proprietors within metro areas of 1 million or more residents.
